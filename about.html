Diksha Tandon

Its couture and perfect blends, refreshing designs and immaculate fittings which make your style speak volumes. We present ourselves as dream weavers for all your needs from ethnic to fusion wear.
New Delhi based designer, her fashion collections strongly reflect a passion for craft & heritage. Her designs are inspired by Indian heritage but with a contemporary vibe.
It was in 2014, that the world first viewed Diksha's design aesthetic with the launch of her label 'Diksha Tandon Designs'. With this she grew from strength to strength gaining a devoted following in the fashion world, which will finally lead to Diksha exhibiting at the India Bridal Fashion Week in 2016.
