Spring/Summer 2016
Udaipur, India


This time our new collection presents textured handlooms juxtaposed with silks and jacquards. Defining the new summer silhouette with shifts, quirky kurtas, concept saris and asymmetrical hemlines,the collection is imbibed with a fresh and youthful simplicity.

Fall/Winter 2015
Varanasi, India


Rules are rewritten on color blocking with the studio’s unique take on the trend. Easy separates that can be paired and include scarves, lungi skirts, dhoti pants, tunics freeing the legs with dynamic and breezy dhoti pants and lungi skirts. Appliqué and chikankari along with kantha and dori embroidery, all find equal footing.
This season they have focussed on shades of marsala, beige and grey with accents of mango sorbets, peaches, tangerines and azure blues. Supporting this narrative are swathes of sushi voile, georgettes, cotton jacquards, cotton silks crepes and cutwork jamdanis from Banaras.
